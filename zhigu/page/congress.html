大会简介

Forum focus

本次大会将围绕“中国制造2025”和“互联网+”战略，汇聚国内外特别是国内在人工智能、智能机器人和大数据领域的产、学、研、用领先机构，交流科研和产业化进展及信息，探索建立核心技术研发攻关、成果转化和产业化平台的机制。大会将邀请政府部门领导、业界领袖、国内外专家学者、行业领军人物、创业者和投融资机构参会。会议设一个主论坛和三个分论坛，预计规模为500人。

The conference will focus on "2025 Chinese manufacturing" and "Internet plus" strategy, the convergence of domestic and abroad in artificial intelligence, intelligent robot and big data in the field of production, learning and research, with leading institutions, development and exchange of information and industrialization of scientific research, explore the establishment of mechanism of core technology research and achievements and industrialization platform. The General Assembly will be invited to the government department leaders, industry leaders, experts and scholars at home and abroad, industry leaders, entrepreneurs and investment and financing institutions. The meeting set up a main forum and three sub forums, is expected to scale to 500 people.
